Free Listing Package

The Free Listing Package is our entry-level listing option designed for software publishers who are looking for a cost-effecitve, no-frills product promotion solution. Completely free of charge, your product will be listed on CNET Download.com and its promotional network while also enjoying these benefits:

  • Placement within CNET Download.com and the rest of our promotional network - which generates visibility to over two million downloaders a day
  • Free HTTP hosting saves you money and ensures the safest download experience available
  • Free product modifications allow you to update your listing as your product evolves
  • Account management through a central dashboard for quickly adding or updating products and selecting services
  • 15-day processing (1-business day processing can be added for $249 at checkout)

Your product will be added to our directory and appear by listing date. In order to keep your product listed as one of the top listings in its sub-category, you can always join our Pay-Per-Download program. We realize that this might have a significant impact on your business plans. In these instances, we recommend upgrading to a Basic Listing (5-day processing) or purchasing Speedy Processing for next business day service.
