Account Info - DatabasePlus
This page allows you to update your account information.


Edit the following fields to change your information:

Account details:

  • Account Name - The account name used to log into DatabasePlus.
  • New Password - If you want to change your password, enter it here and in the Confirm Password field.
  • Confirm Password - See above.
  • First Name - Your first, or given name.
  • Last Name - Your last, or surname.
  • E-mail Address - The e-mail address used for communication to you within DatabasePlus.

Additional options:

  • Yes, I would like to be included in the workgroup feature
  • Yes, I would like to be notified of new services, features, and special offers by e-mail

Click Submit to save your changes.
