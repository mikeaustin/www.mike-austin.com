The Navigation Bar - DatabasePlus  
The navigation bar allows you to quickly jump between the authoring and browsing your tables, views, and reports.  

Here is a sample picture of the navigation bar: (scroll right to view the entire image)		  




Click on the Task, Type and Name pull-down menus to navigate through your items.

  • Task - Switches between task modes. The two available task modes are:
    • Author - Allows you to modify settings, fields, access rights, table events, and to publish reports.
    • Browse - Allows you to add, browse, search, replace, modify, sort, import and export records.
  • Types - Switches between item types. The four available types are:
    • Database - Displays actions related to the database.
    • Tables - Displays actions related to tables.
    • Views - Displays actions related to views.
    • Reports - Displays actions related to reports.
  • Name - Switches between items, such as a table, view or report.
