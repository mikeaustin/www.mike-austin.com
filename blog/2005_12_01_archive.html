[The EA workaround for this is to edit this key in the registry :

HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersionProgramFilesDir

It points to "C:\Program Files" by default.

If you change the value to "E:\Program Files", the patch will look for 3000 MB free space on drive E instead of C.]
