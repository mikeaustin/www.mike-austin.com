Login Help

Logging In

If you want an account, sign up now. It's free. You will automatically be logged in when you have successfully created an account.

If you have an account, enter your account name in the Username field. Enter your password in the Password field. Click Go. Login now.

Remember Me: If you'd appreciate not having to login each time you visit www.bitlocker.com, click the 'Remember Me' check box. The site will automatically log you in in the future.

Use Secure Login: If you want to login using SSL (secure socket layer cryptography techniques for Internet security), click the 'Use Secure Login' check box. The site will use this protocol when you login. Note: The 'Remember Me' function can not be used with Secure Login. Secure Login may increase access time.

Forgotten Username or Password

If you've forgotten your username and/or your password, send email to support@bitlocker.com with 'username password' in the subject field. Please include your full name in the body of the email. In most cases, you'll receive a response the same business day.
