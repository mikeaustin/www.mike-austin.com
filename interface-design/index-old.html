Interface Design

I have been interested in visual design ever since I was a kid. I remember drawing things such as semi-trucks, tanks and VCRs as a kid rather than animals or people. I liked thinking about where to place pieces not just for visual appearance, but also for function. I developed my first interface skills while running an Amiga BBS system in the early nineties, and today I'm working on a research interface called Inertia.

Projects
